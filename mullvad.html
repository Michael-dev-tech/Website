Weaponizing HTTP/3

Connect the dots. The only way the Chinese government can block Mullvad now is to block all HTTP/3 traffic. If they do that, they instantly nuke their own banking sector, e-commerce, and state infrastructure. Mullvad essentially took China's digital economy hostage just to keep your connection alive.
